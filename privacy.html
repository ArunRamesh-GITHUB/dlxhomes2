Privacy Policy

DLX Ltd ("DLX", "we", "us") collects personal data when you submit the eligibility form on this website. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can ask us to delete it.

What we collect

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your postcode
  • Your current heating type
  • Your preferred time to be called

Why we collect it

We use this information to contact you by phone or text to discuss your eligibility for the government Boiler Upgrade Scheme, and, where relevant, to pass your details to an MCS-certified installer partner who can carry out the work. We do not use your data for any other purpose, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as reasonably necessary to progress your enquiry, or until you ask us to delete it.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. To make a request, call us on 07438 903173 or email us using the contact details below, quoting your name and phone number so we can find your record.
